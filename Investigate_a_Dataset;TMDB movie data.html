Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

In this project, we are analysing the TMDB movie data inorder to find trends in movie budgets and revenue in relation to user ratings.

Question(s) for Analysis¶

  1. Is there correlation between vote counts and release year of movies?
  2. Does the budget of a movie affect the revenue generated by that movie?
